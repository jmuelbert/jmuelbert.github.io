# Hello, I'm Jürgen!

Welcome to my personal corner of the internet. I am a **solo open-source developer** and hobbyist coder. After spending years in the tech industry, I now focus on building structures for fun, automating workflows, and sharing what I learn along the way.

## What you'll find here

This documentation is a collection of my thoughts, project standards, and the occasional deep dive into technical topics. I’ve organized everything into a few main categories:

- **Automation:** My philosophy on creating workflows that reduce human error.
- **Standards:** Consistent documentation rules and project structures that make scaling easier.
- **DevLog:** Behind-the-scenes looks at what I’m currently hacking on.
- **Showcase:** Finished projects and demos of my hobby work.

## Why I build in the open

I believe that the best way to learn is to share. Whether it's a small script or a larger framework, I try to keep my work transparent and accessible. No corporate buzzwords, just pure passion for technology and clean code.

> "The best code is the one written for the joy of creating something new."

## My Core Principles

1.  **Don't click on old icons:** Keep moving forward and embrace modern tools like Astro and Starlight.
2.  **Don't look for errors where there don't have to be any:** If the system works and follows the standard, trust the process.
